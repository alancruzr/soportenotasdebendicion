‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new promise note?

Open the app and tap the "+" or "New Note" button. Write your reflection, gratitude, or spiritual thought, and save it. You can add dates and categories to better organize your notes.

Are my notes private?

Yes, absolutely. All your notes are stored securely on your device. We don't share or access your personal content. Your privacy and the intimacy of your spiritual reflections are our priority.

Can I backup my notes?

Yes, you can sync your notes with iCloud to keep them safe and access them from all your Apple devices. Make sure to enable iCloud sync in the app settings.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No, you can use Pocket Promises completely offline. All main features are available without needing an internet connection.

How can I delete a note?

Swipe the note to the left in the main list and select "Delete", or open the note and look for the delete option in the menu.
